Japanese Furikake Salmon

Furikake Salmon is a traditional dish from Japan

Ingredients

  • Atlantic salmon
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • garlic powder
  • shoyu (soy sauce)
  • Furikake
  • Black pepper

Recipe

  • Prepare salmon and oven
  • Make Kewpie shoyu sauce
  • Prepare salmon
  • Add the shoyu mayo and furikake
  • Bake the salmon
